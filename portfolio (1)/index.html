About the Designer


Hello, thank you for visiting, this is page will constantly be updated as I complete new projects. A little bit about me, I am originally from Clevelenad, OH (small plug for the current NBA Champs!!). I did my undegraduate degree at The State University of New York at Buffalo. There I majored in Computer Engineering with a minor in Electrical Engineering, I also played division one basketball. While in college my basketball career took off, by the time I graduated with my degree in Engineering I was one of the top player in the country. I took the once in a lifetime opportunity to play professional basketball in Spain. I was able to travel the world and live out a dream.

After my basketball career I got into coaching, giving back to a sport that afforded me so many opportunities. I am currently working in higher education as I finish my Master's Degree in Electrical Engineering, which I will recieve in March 2017. I am a self starter and self taught web developer.

Skills: HTML5, CSS, jQuery, Bootstrap3, Python, C++, Microsoft Office, Teamwork, Leadership
